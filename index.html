The Zena Mission
Empowering Brand Managers
Today’s brand managers face a growing challenge.
They’re often stymied by too much data, too few
insights, too many tasks…and not enough resources to bring it all together.

Leveraging decades of brand management
expertise, we set out to help solve this dilemma.
We partnered with AI/ML software experts to
create a “virtual assistant” platform that was
custom designed for the needs of CPG brand
managers. The result was Zena, a transformative
resource that expands every brand manager’s
capabilities.

Zena was designed by brand managers, for brand managers.
The Zena Advantage

Zena expands your
"brand intelligence."

  • Uploads, organizes and stores all of your brand’s data, history and relevant information.
  • Examines large swaths of data to help find undiscovered patterns.
  • Helps you transform vast datasets into actionable insights.
  • The more you use Zena, the smarter Zena gets about your brand and your way!

Zena interacts with you.

  • Zena is on constant standby, ready and able to answer your requests and questions.
  • Ask Zena about your market data, brand performance metrics, consumer research and more.
  • Zena responds directly to your prompts and keeps a history of its discussions with you.

Zena saves you time!

  • Performs labor-intensive tasks in a fraction of the usual time.
  • Zena unlocks the full potential of your data while saving time in the process.
  • Zena multiplies every brand manager’s capabilities.
Zena is customized to your brand.
Zena is built specifically for your use,
because it’s engineered from your brand’s data.
