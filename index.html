The Zena Mission
Empowering Brand Managers
Today’s brand managers face challenge: they are often stymied by too much data, too few insights, too many tasks...and not enough resources to bring it all together.
Leveraging decades of our brand management expertise, we set out to solve this dilemma. Harnessing the emerging power of artificial intelligence, we partnered with AI/ ML software experts to create a “virtual assistant” platform that was custom designed for the needs of CPG brand managers. The result was Zena, a transformative resource that expands every brand manager’s strategic, analytical and executional capabilities.
As we like to say: “Zena was designed by brand managers, for brand managers.”
